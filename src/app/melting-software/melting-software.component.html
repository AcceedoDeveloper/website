Melting Hardware

The Melting Hardware device is designed to monitor and optimize machine operations in industrial settings. It accurately detects which operator is running the machine at any given time. The device also tracks the quantity of materials used during the production process. It identifies how many types of materials are involved in each production cycle..

Additionally, the system records productivity levels to help improve overall efficiency. Rejection rates are monitored, allowing quick detection of defects and reducing waste. With its comprehensive tracking features, the Melting Hardware device supports better decision-making and operational control.

Melting Software

Our software is the perfect complement to the Melting Hardware device, offering an intuitive interface to view and manage critical operational data. It displays real-time insights into machine performance, including operator identification and the materials used during production.

The software’s advanced analytics feature helps users track productivity and identify inefficiencies, providing actionable insights for improvements. Rejection and defect data are visualized clearly, enabling quick responses to reduce waste and maintain quality standards. With its powerful reporting and data visualization tools, the software transforms complex data into easy-to-understand graphics and charts.

It empowers businesses to optimize resources, improve operational efficiency, and maintain high standards in production. Seamlessly integrated with the Melting Hardware device, the software ensures that all production parameters are accessible in a centralized, user-friendly platform, driving better productivity and operational control.
