Melting Software

Our melting software optimizes the production process by managing raw materials, alloy additions, and temperature monitoring without human contact. It automates planning, furnace selection, and charge-mix composition, providing real-time data access on mobile or computer. Key features include spectrum analysis, automatic reports, stock updates, and furnace maintenance tracking for efficient and high-quality production.

Our system software enables the professional to manage the raw material quantity, addition of alloys, spectrum result and monitor the temperature during the melting process without the need for human contact in the physical process. User can view live data anywhere on their mobile or computer.

Advantages of melting software

Spectrum result taken from machine and compared to the calculated values. Maintain alloys and rawmaterial stocks up to date.

Reports are generated automatically based on the plans completed. Melting furnace logs and its capacity recorded. The priority and charge-mix addition of each product can be changed at anywhere.

The cost and quantity of alloys and raw materials are compared with previous loads. Automatically extract the alloy addition quantity from spectrum result.

key features of melting software

Planning production for customer with alloy composition and planned date. User can also change the product priority based on the customer requirements. Once the product is planned, it awaits furnace selection and chargemix composition.

Available furnaces are listed for selection and the raw materials for the product are selected by the operator for loading. The charge-mix amount is automatically calculated and the required amount of pure alloy is displayed. Furnace lining, Ladle lining and heat number are recorded for better maintenance.

A final report is generated to display requirements and actual data at every step of melting process, product produced in which furnace and its alloy composition and results of all tests performed on parts in the work order. Standard printers can be used to print out all required information. Support for data conversion to popular spreadsheet formats is also provided.
