Melting Software

Our advanced melting software automates and optimizes the entire metal production process—from raw material management to alloy composition—ensuring high-quality output with minimal human intervention.

Partnering

Our melting software optimizes the production process by managing raw materials, alloy additions, and temperature monitoring without human contact. It automates planning, furnace selection, and charge-mix composition, providing real-time data access on mobile or computer.

Our system software enables the professional to manage the raw material quantity, addition of alloys, spectrum result and monitor the temperature during the melting process without the need for human contact in the physical process.

Planning

Our Industrial IoT software integrates machines, sensors, and cloud computing to streamline production, enhance efficiency, and reduce waste. It provides real-time data, predictive maintenance, and smart planning, optimizing resources and minimizing human effort. Key features include automated data collection, machine integration, and seamless reporting for better performance tracking.

It simplifies onboarding, salary components, and attendance data management, boosting productivity and accuracy.
