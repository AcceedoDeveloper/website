Software Overview

Each furnace shows a LIVE status indicator, confirming active monitoring and availability for real-time updates.

Dashboard

It displays real-time data on bin actual weights of key elements and provides individual status panels for each furnace. Users can schedule, start, or cancel plans, monitor lining statuses, and create charge mixes. With quick navigation to related modules, it ensures streamlined control and visibility across the entire melting process.

Reports

Generate detailed melting process reports including production output, rejection rates, and material composition. These reports help track performance trends, identify issues early, and support data-driven decision making for quality and efficiency improvements.

Melting Planning


Plan production based on customer requirements, including alloy composition and scheduled delivery dates. Easily prioritize products to meet urgent or changing needs. Adjust priorities in real time as customer demands evolve. Once planning is complete, the system awaits furnace assignment. Users can proceed to select the appropriate furnace. Next, define the charge mix composition for melting.

Furnace selection and chargemix composition


Operators can view and select available furnaces for production. Raw materials are chosen based on the product requirements. The system auto-calculates the charge-mix and required pure alloy quantity. Furnace lining, ladle lining, and heat number are recorded. These records help maintain quality, traceability, and efficient maintenance.

Our melting software is designed to collect real-time data during the melting process in the foundry, ensuring accurate tracking of temperature, weight, and material composition. It helps operators monitor and control every stage of the melt, improving consistency and reducing errors.

By displaying this data in a user-friendly interface, the software enhances decision-making, supports quality control, and ensures traceability of each batch for improved operational efficiency.

Melting Hardware

The Melting Hardware device is designed to monitor and optimize machine operations in industrial settings. It accurately detects which operator is running the machine at any given time. The device also tracks the quantity of materials used during the production process. It identifies how many types of materials are involved in each production cycle..

Additionally, the system records productivity levels to help improve overall efficiency. Rejection rates are monitored, allowing quick detection of defects and reducing waste. With its comprehensive tracking features, the Melting Hardware device supports better decision-making and operational control.
