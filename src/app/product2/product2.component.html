Payroll & Attendance Management System

Payroll & Attendance Management System is software designed to simplify attendance tracking and salary calculation for industries and businesses. With real-time monitoring and automated reports, managing your workforce has never been easier.

Industries We Serve

Manufacturing Industries:

Streamline attendance tracking across multiple shifts and large worker groups on the factory floor, including contract-based employees.

Commercial Businesses:

Manage staff attendance and automate payroll in retail stores, showrooms, and service-based businesses, with support for contract workers.

Offices with Shift-Based Employees:

Handle flexible working hours, multiple shifts, and accurate salary reports for corporate, admin teams, and contract-based employees.

Secure Individual Login

Every employee is provided with a unique login username and password to securely access their payroll & attendance from anywhere and they can view the history of leave ,OD and Permissions

Easy Leave Application

Employees can easily apply for Leave, On Duty (OD), and Permission requests directly through the software,making the entire process simple, fast, and hassle-free without the need for paperwork or manual approval rounds.

Real-Time Status Tracking

All leave and permission applications are processed in real-time, enabling employees to instantly track the status of their requests and receive timely notifications once they are approved or rejected

Calender View


The calendar-view offers a clear, visual layout of daily attendance, making it easy to track presence, absences, and holidays at a glance. Users can navigate through months and quickly identify attendance patterns. This intuitive interface enhances planning and ensures better workforce oversight.

Report Table


Our attendance system provides real-time tracking and detailed reports of employee presence, ensuring accuracy and accountability. It simplifies workforce management by generating automated summaries for daily, weekly, and monthly attendance records.
