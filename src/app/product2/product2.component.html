Attendance System

Attendance System is a software designed to simplify attendance tracking and salary calculation in industries and businesses. With real-time monitoring and automated reports, managing your workforce has never been easier.

Industries We Serve

Manufacturing Industries:

Streamline attendance tracking across multiple shifts and large worker groups on the factory floor.

Commercial Businesses:

Manage staff attendance and automate payroll in retail stores, showrooms, and service-based businesses.

Offices with Shift-Based Employees:

Handle flexible working hours, multiple shifts, and accurate salary reports for corporate and admin teams.

Secure Individual Login

Every employee is provided with a unique login username and password to securely access their attendance calender from anywhere.

Easy Leave Application

Employees can easily apply for Leave, On Duty (OD), and Permission requests directly through the software,making the entire process simple, fast, and hassle-free without the need for paperwork or manual approval rounds.

Real-Time Status Tracking

All leave and permission applications are processed in real-time, enabling employees to instantly track the status of their requests and receive timely notifications once they are approved or rejected
