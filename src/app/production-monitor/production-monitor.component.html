Why Choose Production Monitor?

Real-Time Decision Making

The Production Monitor provides real-time data on machine output and operator performance. This allows supervisors to detect issues instantly and take corrective actions without delays. It improves workflow efficiency and reduces production downtime significantly.

Quality Control and Optimization

By tracking rejection rates and time-based trends, the system ensures consistent product quality. It highlights underperforming machines or shifts, enabling data-driven improvements. This leads to optimized resource usage and better overall productivity.

Machine-wise production tracks the output of each machine individually to identify performance differences. It helps optimize operations by highlighting high-performing machines and those needing attention.

Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE) is a key performance indicator used in manufacturing to measure the efficiency and effectiveness of a production process.

Availability

The percentage of scheduled time the machine is actually running (uptime vs downtime).

Performance

How fast the machine runs compared to its maximum speed (actual production rate vs ideal rate).

Quality

The proportion of good units produced compared to total units (rejects and defects impact this).
