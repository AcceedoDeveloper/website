Our Story

From Coimbatore to the World

Headquartered in Coimbatore, Acceedo is a dynamic software company providing cutting-edge web design, development, and application services. We specialize in Multilevel Marketing software solutions and have served both corporate and small business enterprises with customized web-related services.

Today, we're at the forefront of Industrial IoT innovation, deploying connected devices and machinery that revolutionize how industries operate. Our solutions enable real-time data collection, machine health monitoring, performance analytics, and actionable insights for management.

By harnessing machine learning, we've developed systems that can predict potential failures before they occur, saving companies millions in operational costs and downtime.
