Heat Treatment Monitor

Advanced real-time monitoring system for precise heat treatment processes, ensuring optimal material properties and operational efficiency.

Partnering

Project Last Mile works with a national Ministry of Health in enhancing the work they have already done in improving the health system capacity. Heat Treatment Monitor tracks energy consumption for every cycle, providing detailed data on power usage. This helps identify energy-saving opportunities and ensures efficient operation. Monitoring energy per cycle also aids in cost analysis and sustainability efforts.

Planning
